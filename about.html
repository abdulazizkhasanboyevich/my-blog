About me

A little about who I am, what I do, and why I write.

Hello, I'm Alex

I'm a developer and writer based somewhere between a coffee shop and a quiet desk at home. I build things on the web, think too much about typography, and believe the best ideas often arrive when you're not looking for them.

This blog is my corner of the internet — a place to share what I'm learning, what I'm questioning, and the occasional story that doesn't fit anywhere else. No algorithms, no ads, just words.

When I'm not writing, you'll find me on long walks, experimenting in the kitchen, or reading something that's slightly too old to still be on the bestseller list.
